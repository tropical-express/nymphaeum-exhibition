# Roman Nymphaeum Archive

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-orange)](LICENSE)

Digital museum archive documenting Roman nymphaea, monumental fountains, and sacred water architecture throughout the ancient Mediterranean world.

---

## 🏛️ About

The Roman Nymphaeum Archive is a static museum-style website focused on:

- Roman water monuments
- Classical fountain architecture
- Civic water engineering
- Sacred and ceremonial spaces
- Ancient Mediterranean archaeology

The project is designed as a digital exhibit system inspired by museum collections and archival presentation.

---

## 📚 Current Exhibits

### Jerash Nymphaeum
Monumental Roman fountain structure located in Jerash, Jordan.

### Side Nymphaeum
Large Anatolian Roman fountain complex located in Side, Turkey.

### Trajan Fountain
Imperial Roman fountain monument located in Ephesus, Turkey.
